The Bartrams

A Father of American Botany & Exploration of North American Flora

-Carl Linnaeus

John Bartram was born to a Quaker farm family in colonial Pennsylvania on March 23, 1699. He was an early American Botanist, horticulurist, and explorer. In short, Bartram was the first and most badass botanist in early America, who is soley responsible for the introduction of many new world plants to the western world.

Badass Claims to Botanical Fame

  • Wrote 'Observations on the Inhabitants, Climate, Soil, Rivers, Productions, Animals, and other Materrers Worthy of Notice' after visiting the shores of Lake Ontario
  • One of the first practicing botanists in North America
  • Employed by King George III as the King's Botanist of North America
  • Was a co-founder of the American Philosophical Society, with Benjamin Franklin, in 1743
  • Bore William Bartam, who succeeded his father in being a fucking BADASS botanist
